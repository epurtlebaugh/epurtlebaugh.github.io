About Us

At Arek's Lawncare Services (ALS), we take pride in transforming your outdoor spaces into beautiful, healthy landscapes. Founded by Arek, a passionate horticulturist with over 10 years of experience, our mission is to provide reliable, professional lawn care that exceeds your expectations. Whether it’s routine mowing, seasonal cleanups, or custom landscaping projects, we’re committed to delivering high-quality, personalized service that leaves your lawn looking its best all year round.
