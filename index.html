Our Story

At Tito's Empanadas, we believe every bite tells a story. Our journey began in the vibrant streets of Buenos Aires, where Tito, a passionate chef with a heart full of culinary dreams, spent his childhood helping his grandmother in her bustling kitchen. She taught him the art of crafting empanadas, sharing secret family recipes that had been passed down through generations. Each empanada was a labor of love, filled with fresh ingredients and a dash of nostalgia, capturing the spirit of Argentina in every savory pocket.


In 2010, Tito brought his passion to life by opening Tito's Empanadas in the heart of the city. His mission was simple: to create a warm, inviting space where people could gather and savor the authentic flavors of Argentina. From traditional beef and chicken to creative vegetarian options, each empanada is made from scratch, reflecting Tito's commitment to quality and tradition. Today, Tito's Empanadas is not just a restaurant; it's a celebration of heritage, community, and the joy of sharing good food with friends and family. Come join us and experience the taste of Argentina right here at home!
